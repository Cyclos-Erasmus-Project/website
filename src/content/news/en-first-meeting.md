The CYCLOS consortium is pleased to announce the first transnational partnership meeting, scheduled to take place in France later this autumn.

## Meeting Objectives

The kick-off meeting will bring together representatives from all four partner organizations to:

- Formally launch project activities
- Establish detailed timelines for each work package
- Define roles and responsibilities
- Plan the first round of educational content development
- Discuss the digital platform requirements

## Work Package Overview

The meeting will focus on coordinating the five main work packages:

- **WP1**: Project Management
- **WP2**: Cycling Knowledge
- **WP3**: From Theory to Practice
- **WP4**: Digital Platform
- **WP5**: Dissemination & Evaluation

## Building Connections

Beyond the formal agenda, this meeting represents an important opportunity for partners to build personal connections and establish the collaborative spirit that will drive the project's success over the next 30 months.

More details about the meeting outcomes will be shared in future updates.
